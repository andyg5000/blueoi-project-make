Medical ID Fashions Product Attributes Display Overrides
========================================================

Updates Commerce Carts Views display for handling product attributes, completely removes product title.

At every targeted product type
admin/commerce/products/types/%product-types%/display

ie..
admin/commerce/products/types/bracelets/display

Turn on : CUSTOM DISPLAY SETTINGS "Attribute View"

goto 
admin/commerce/products/types/bracelets/display/attribute_view

Update Desired Information to be added; in this case I only added 

Fractions Pricing	
Inch Pricing
